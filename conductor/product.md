# Initial Concept
A Flutter e-commerce application integrated with Supabase, designed to provide a high-quality mobile shopping experience.

## Vision
To build a fast, secure, and user-friendly mobile application that connects shoppers with products through a seamless digital experience.

## Target Users
- **Individual Shoppers:** Looking for a mobile-first experience to discover and purchase products quickly and securely.
- **Guest Users:** Who want to browse the product catalog and explore the app before committing to an account.

## Core Goals
- **Seamless Mobile Experience:** Deliver a fast and responsive application that feels native and intuitive.
- **Secure and Diverse Payments:** Ensure user trust by offering reliable and secure transaction methods.
- **Robust Product Discovery:** Implement powerful search and filtering to help users find exactly what they're looking for.

## Key Features
- **Comprehensive User Authentication:** Supporting standard sign-up/log-in along with modern biometric authentication for enhanced security and convenience.
- **Full Product Catalog:** Dynamic grid and list views with detailed product descriptions and search capabilities.
- **Advanced Shopping Cart and Checkout:** An intuitive flow for managing items, selecting addresses, and completing payments.
- **Order History and Tracking:** Allowing users to review past purchases and stay updated on their current order status.
- **Favorites and Wishlist:** Personalized spaces for users to save products they're interested in for future purchase.
